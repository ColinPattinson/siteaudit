      🏛️ UK Government Service Standard

      The 14-point UK Government Service Standard defines what good digital public services look like. Each point is scored out of 10 with a rationale, benchmarked against GOV.UK.

      ♿ WCAG 2.2 Accessibility

      WCAG 2.2 Level AA is the legal requirement for all UK public sector websites under PSBAR 2018. GOV.UK scores 91/100. Failure to meet this standard is a legal risk and excludes disabled users.

      🔒 Privacy & Security

      UK public sector websites must comply with UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, and PECR — including HTTPS, cookie consent, a named DPO and transparent data handling. GOV.UK scores 85/100.

      🌱 Environmental Sustainability

      The GDS Technology Code of Practice asks services to consider environmental impact — hosting carbon commitments, page weight, image optimisation and third-party scripts. GOV.UK scores 72/100.
